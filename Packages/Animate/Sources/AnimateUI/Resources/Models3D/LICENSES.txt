Format Notes
------------
All models are in GLB (binary glTF) format. SceneKit can load these
via ModelIO (MDLAsset) or by converting to SCN at build time.

For native USDZ models, Apple provides samples at:
  https://developer.apple.com/augmented-reality/quick-look/
  (Note: Apple's models are 4-14MB each, photorealistic quality)
