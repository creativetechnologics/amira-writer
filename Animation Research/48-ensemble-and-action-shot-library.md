# 48 — Ensemble and Action Shot Library

Date: 2026-03-31

## Purpose
Expand the shot library into higher-pressure scenarios beyond solo and duet coverage.

## Ensemble dialogue semicircle
- category: ensemble_dialogue
- likely routing: hybrid
- reason: several characters visible, but only a subset may need detailed mouth acting; a shared lighting plan is required so face readability stays coherent across the group

## Group walking shot
- category: group_walk
- likely routing: hybrid
- reason: reusable loops possible, but staging coordination and shared scene lighting rise quickly

## Action insert with hero focus
- category: action_insert
- likely routing: hybrid or ai-video-fallback depending on camera intensity
- lighting note: high-contrast practicals or firelight can push this toward fallback unless the lighting engine already supports that profile well

## Transition chase wide
- category: chase_wide
- likely routing: ai-video-fallback
- reason: camera and environmental complexity dominate

## Market crowd with featured leads
- category: crowd_leads
- likely routing: hybrid
- reason: leads may remain internal while crowd/background motion simplifies

## Guideline
As action density, character count, and lighting complexity rise, the system should bias away from a pure package-driven route unless the shot reuses already-proven loops and lighting profiles.
