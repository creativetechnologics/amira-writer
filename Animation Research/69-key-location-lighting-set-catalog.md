# 69 — Key-Location Lighting Set Catalog

Date: 2026-03-31

## Purpose
Define reusable lighting sets for the most likely high-usage hero locations in Amira.

## Priority locations

### 1. District clinic exterior
Why high priority:
- Luke medic scenes
- Amira family/clinic-adjacent dialogue
- likely repeated across day, sunset, and night

Recommended reusable profiles:
- `daylight_soft`
- `sunset_warm`
- `moonlight_blue`
- `dust_storm_flat`

### 2. Clinic interior fluorescent room
Why high priority:
- medical scenes
- dialogue scenes
- emotional close-ups

Recommended reusable profiles:
- `fluorescent_clinic`
- `tungsten_interior`
- `night_practical_mix`

### 3. Rooftop at sunset
Why high priority:
- hero dialogue/singing staging
- emotionally significant silhouette-driven scenes

Recommended reusable profiles:
- `sunset_warm`
- `dusk_desaturated`
- `moonlight_blue`

### 4. Village street at night
Why high priority:
- travel / tension / walk-and-talk scenes
- mixed practical lighting opportunities

Recommended reusable profiles:
- `moonlight_blue`
- `night_practical_mix`
- `firelight_flicker`

### 5. Family courtyard / home exterior
Why high priority:
- Amira-centered civilian scenes
- softer emotional scenes with practical sources

Recommended reusable profiles:
- `daylight_soft`
- `sunset_warm`
- `night_practical_mix`

## Selection rule
These key locations should be treated as the first reusable location-lighting library for hero-focused production work.
