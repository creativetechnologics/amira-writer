# 41 — Duet, Group, and Transition Routing Cases

Date: 2026-03-31

## Purpose
Expand routing guidance beyond solo shots.

## Duet two-shot
Usually hybrid or internal depending on:
- mouth package coverage for both characters
- camera complexity
- overlap / occlusion pressure

## Group blocking scene
Often hybrid when:
- 3–5 characters speak or sing in the same composition
- only leads need precise mouth acting
- supporting/background figures can simplify

## Transition montage shot
Often hybrid or AI-video fallback when:
- style and motion density matter more than exact retakes
- package precision is less important than atmosphere

## Group spectacle wide
Usually AI-video fallback unless the shot is highly controlled and repeats existing loops.

## Rule
As character count rises, the burden shifts from package precision to staging/complexity management.
